MOMENTUM MENTAL HEALTH AWARDS

BRAND IDENTITY, PRINT AND EVENT DESIGN

Momentum Walk-In Counselling was in need of a brand identity and various print and electronic deliverables for the 2018 Momentum Awards, the inaugural celebration of Edmonton’s mental health community. The awards brand plays off of Momentum’s existing colours, and focuses on three main themes: the brain, the helping hand and the speech bubble.
